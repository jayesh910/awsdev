Introduction to KMS:
  - Managed: Managed service for you to make & use of encryption keys.
  - Integrated: with many aws services as easy as checking box
  - Simple: to encrypt data with encryption keys
  
Uses: Sensitive data to keep secret, customer data, financial data, database passwords, secrets, credentials.

Integrates with S3, RDS, DynamoDB, Lambda, EBS, EFS, cloudtrail, developer tools.

Customer Master Key: Encrypt/Decrypt data upto 4KB. It is used to generate / encrypt / decrypt data key.
Data Key: Used to encrypt/decrypt your data.
This is called as envelope encryption.
